HC provider hcpr glims yes yes Normal

All health care providers are correspondents and therefore inherit the capabilities defined for correspondents. In a medical environment, a HC Provider could typically be the attending physician from an encounter. Health care providers usually require a health care code. This code may change in time due to additionally acquired professional skills.
