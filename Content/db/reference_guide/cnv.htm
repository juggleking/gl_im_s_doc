Conversion cnv glims yes yes Normal

A conversion record allows GLIMS to compare numerical results expressed in units of different dimensions, under restricted conditions.

Fields

Coefficient

The Coefficient and Term are chosen so that the following applies:
Result.Value(DimensionOut.BaseUnit) =
DimensionIn.Coefficient * Result.Value(DimensionIn.BaseUnit) + DimensionIn.Term

In other words, the Coefficient and Term make sure that the results expressed in their alternate units, multiplied by their coefficients and possibly added/substracted with their respective terms match. For example, converting degrees Celsius in Kelvin measurement requires a Coefficient of 1 and a term of -273 or 273.
