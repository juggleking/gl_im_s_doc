This table holds the budgets per correspondent, per period and per budget class. Budget calculation is defined by BudgetDefinitions. A Budget is made up of BudgetInvoices.

Fields

Amount

Total of the amounts of all the BudgetInvoices on this Budget. Only BudgetInvoices that are marked 'relevant' are taken into account.

Mandatory Automatic Fractional Amount

The budget class of this budget

Last update time

Automatic DateTime LastUpdateTime

Owner

The correspondent for whom this budget is calculated.

Mandatory Invariant Correspondent Owner

Period

Defines the period for the budget. The period starts at the date specified by the startdate field. The external date of the BudgetInvoices on this Budget must fall in this period.

Mandatory Invariant InvoiceGroupingPeriod Period

One of: Daily, Weekly, Half monthly, Monthly, Quarterly, Per4Months, Half yearly, Yearly

Points

Total of the points of all the BudgetInvoices on this Budget. Only BudgetInvoices that are marked 'relevant' are taken into account.

Mandatory Automatic Fractional Points

Start date

This field indicates the start date of the budget.

Mandatory Invariant Date StartDate
