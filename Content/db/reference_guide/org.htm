Organization org glims yes yes Normal

Organizations are non-individual correspondents of a type not explicitly implemented in the other subtypes. They can be organized hierarchically. This hierarchy allows for example that individual certificates are sent to an organization's local department, in contrast to the corresponding invoices which may be sent to the organization's central department. Cyclic organization definitions should be prevented.
