This table is used for printing a billing document; it serves as the start table for the text that makes up a discount table detail line. A detail line is printed for each discount in the document. Aditionally a line with the discount totals is printed.

Fields

Base amount

Total amount on which the discount is calculated.
Equals:
- When printing invoice summary details: The total amount on which the discount was calculated (invs_Amount).
- When printing FinancialShipments: The sum of all invoice summary amounts (invs_Amount) for this DiscountGroup.

Fractional BaseAmount

Discount

The total discount amount granted for the associated discount. It was calculated with the 'Amount' field as basis.

Fractional Discount

Discount group formula

Reference to the discount group formula used for calculating this discount. Only filled in when available, never filled in on the financial shipment level

DiscountGroupFormula DiscountGroupFormula

Invoice summary

Reference to the invoice summary. For easy access, only filled in on the invoice summary level

InvoiceSummary InvoiceSummary

Is total line

Indicates that this line contains the total of all discounts. If so, references like DiscountGroupFormula are not filled in.

Logical IsTotalLine

Number of lines

Contains the total number of discount lines that is printed in this discount table, including the total line.

PositiveInteger NumberOfLines

Seq no

A sequence number, starting by 1 and increased for each discount printed. The order in which discount lines are printed is determined by the SeqNo field of the DiscountGroup table.

PositiveInteger SeqNo
