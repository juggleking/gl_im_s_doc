Discount group formula dsgf glims yes yes Normal

Defines the discount given by a discount group. Each discount group formula has a start amount specifying the minimum invoice summary amount needed for activation of this formula. The formula whose start amount is closest to the invoice summary amount is selected. This mechanism allows for turnover dependent discounts.
Note that each discount group must have a formula with startvalue 0. GLIMS will automatically create such a formula for each new discount group. The formula with startvalue 0 can not be deleted.
The discount granted equals the formula's 'FixedAmount' plus a 'Percentage' of the invoice summary amount above 'StartAmount'.
