The status of an order.
An order is created with status 'Initial'. If at least one result is confirmed it becomes 'Partial'. If all results are validated it reaches 'Complete'. Once its status is (explicitly) 'Closed', it can no longer be changed. 'Complete' orders automatically get status 'Closed' after successful archiving.
Over time all orders should reach the status 'Closed' or 'Discontinued'.
