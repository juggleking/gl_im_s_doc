Type

This field indicates the type of this cash register movement.
Possible types are payment, deposit (in cash register), withdrawal (out of cash register), actual register amount, register opened, register closed.

Mandatory Invariant CashRegisterMovementType MovementType

One of: Payment, Deposit, WithDrawal, ActualBalance, OpenRegister, CloseRegister

Paid amount

This field specifies the paid amount (stored in the cash register) : only filled in case of real payments

Invariant Fractional PaidAmount

Payment

This is the payment referenced by the movement on the account.

Automatic Payment Payment

Returned amount

This field specifies the returned amount (taken out of cash register) : only filled in case of real payments with a returned amount (to the patient during cash payment)

Invariant Fractional ReturnedAmount

Seq no

This field specifies the sequence number of a movement on a firm account (or register) within the same ValueDate (same day).

Mandatory Integer SequenceNo

Status

This field indicates the status of this cash register movement.
Booked means entered movement, validated means validated movement and amount, fixed means amount fixed after calculating end of day amount, discontinued means movement to be ignored.

Mandatory Automatic RegisterMovementStatus Status

One of: Booked, Validated, Fixed, Discontinued
