8.25 Admission status

Supported values:
OP = Discharge -> Close corresponding encounter
IP = Admission Inpatient -> Creation of encounter of type 'I'
ER = Urgency Admission -> Creation of encounter of type 'I'
PA = Pre-admission -> Creation of encounter of type 'I' in the future
MP = Transfer -> Transfer of patient from one ward to another
IO = External of outpatient Admission -> Creation of encounter of type 'A'

If the admission status is not specified, the translator will verify the ward and room field to decide whether the patient is an in- or outpatient.
