How to configure an Edifact copy report in GLIMS:

  • During order entry, you must specify the issuer and copy receiver.
  • For both issuers a separate, issuer-specific default report must be created.  In the default report of the copy receiver, the field 'Is copy' must be enabled.

Example:

Faber is the issuer
Utker is the copy receiver

Two files will be generated:

(*) Multiple OPB segments are possible. They are distinct from each other by an extra sequence number. Returns are not allowed in the comment field - they will force the creation of a new OPB segment.
