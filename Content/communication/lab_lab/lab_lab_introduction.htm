Lab - Lab communication: Introduction

Content: Introduction - Communication concept - Communication protocol

Introduction

The lab - lab communication functionality in Glims consists of outsourcing tests from one laboratory to another.  The idea is to send tests from Lab X to Lab Y and to send the results back from Lab Y to Lab X.  The documentation of the lab lab concept will focus on communication between two (or more) Glims systems although Glims could also communicate to a third party LIS.

Communication concept

Lab X receives the original orders.  Using a selection mechanism, some or all tests of that order will be sent to Lab Y which will execute the actual tests.  When the results for these tests are available in Lab Y, they are sent back to Lab X.

 

Communication protocol

The communication between the laboratories is most commonly based on the HL7 2.5 protocol and uses 4 drivers to handle all messaging.

  • The homx driver exports the tests from Lab X.  The test export is based on action export.
  • The homl driver imports the tests in Lab Y.
  • The houl driver export the results from Lab Y. The result export is based on electronic reports.
  • The houi driver import the results in Lab X.
