Download blood donor info to an instrument

Introduction

Glims can send blood donor information when downloading a crossmatch test to an instrument. The information is sent upon request of the translator.

MIPS ASTM protocol

Query message: Q.15.1.1 - Query specific tag 'DonorInfo'

When O-segment has a blood bag, Glims will send blood bag donor information in a M-records following the O-segment:

M|seq|dnri^DonorInfo|DonorInfoType|data

Where DonorInfoType can be: Product, ProductSpecification, BloodGroup, Antigens.

Product

M|seq|dnri^DonorInfo|Product|code

  • code: BloodBag->BloodProduct.Mnemonic or Station.CodingSytem.CodeSet(BloodProduct).Code.Value

ProductSpecification

M|seq|dnri^DonorInfo|ProductSpecification|code^barcode

  • code: BloodBag->BloodProductSpecification.Mnemonic or Station.CodingSytem.CodeSet(BloodProductSpecification).Code.Value
  • barcode: BloodBag->BloodProductSpecification.Barcode

BloodGroup

M|seq|dnri^DonorInfo|BloodGroup|group^rhesus

  • group: BloodBag.BloodGroup
  • rhesus: BloodBag.BloodRhesus
  • Antigens

M|seq|dnri^DonorInfo|Antigens|summary

  • summary: List of Antigens with a + or -, separated by a blank. Antigen is represented by antigen.Mnemomic or Station.CodingSytem.CodeSet(Antigen).Code.Value
