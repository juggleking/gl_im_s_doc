Invoicing: Invoice summaries

Introduction

An invoice summary in GLIMS is the actual document that is sent to a customer (fund, patient, ..) with a friendly request to pay: so the 'invoice summary' plays the role of the 'real world' invoice. Invoice summaries always consist of one or several invoices for one payer. Invoice summaries group one or more invoices, based on the definitions of the 'invoice grouping type' table.  It is possible to create reminders for invoice summaries and to register payments for them.

Invoice summary creation

Invoice summaries can be created by means of the invoice function 'Invoice summary creation'. Optionally invoices summaries can already be created during the tariffication.
