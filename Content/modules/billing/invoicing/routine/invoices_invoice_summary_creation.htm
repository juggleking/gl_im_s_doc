Invoice.InvoiceSummaryCreation

This function allows grouping invoices on invoice summaries.  In practice, an invoice summary will be created per payer and possibly per period. Invoice summaries for the national health service (Belgium: mutualiteit) are generally created per fund and per month. Patient invoice summaries are created for a shorter period of time or per order.  Note that an existing invoice summary can be reused until it has attained the status 'Fixed'.

Invoice grouping type

The invoice grouping type allows defining how the invoice must be grouped into invoice summaries

Remarks

Payer

By default, the invoice summary payer will be the invoice payer. However, this can be overruled by specifying a payer for the correspondent.

Invoice reimburser versus payer

It is possible that certain invoice summaries, which are normally to be charged at the national health service, (or another third party), have to be paid by the patient (who will be reimbursed). On the other hand the invoice summary has to be printed as a document for the national health service (Belgium: mutualiteitsattest).  This can be obtained by specifying a different reimburser type and payer type in the policy clause. By doing so, the invoice summary will state the patient as payer and the national health service (or another third party) as reimburser.  When printing the invoice summaries, one should then take into account the reimburser type.
