HL7: IN1 - insurance segment

The IN1 segment contains insurance policy coverage information necessary to produce properly pro-rated and patient and insurance.  

Integration of PaymentAgreement if Fund.Mnemonic and Matriculation are specified in the PV1 segment of the HL7 ADT message:  the fund's default policy name will be used as payment agreement policy name. The payment agreement creation functionality will be triggered by a site attribute 'StoreInsuranceData' on the Translator table.

Example: if we are importing patients via the Translator Xcare and we also want payment agreements to be created, a site attribute 'StoreInsuranceData=YES' must be set on the Xcare GLIMS translator record.  Only when the site attribute is set, the insurance data will be imported.
