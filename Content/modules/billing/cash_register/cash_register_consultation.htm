Cash register - Consultation

Details of previous cash register sessions

In the Cash register management screen, the section Previous sessions shows all closed sessions of a cash register.

Double-click a session to view its details.

Cash register session browsers

GLIMS offers two ways to consult the cash register sessions. From both browsers, you can use the contextual menu / ribbon item Print to print a cash register report.

All cash register sessions of a particular cash register

Select a Cash register and choose the contextual menu / ribbon item Cash register sessions.

All cash register sessions (sorted by opening time)

From the main menu, choose Start -> Billing -> Cash register sessions to get a list of all sessions sorted by opening time.
