Identifications

The Identifications table is used for all official identifications of 'Kassen', 'Labs', etc.. In order for the KV-DT billing to work correct, make sure that the correct identifications are filled in. Note that official identifications are those that are assigned by the correspondent specified in ssit_HCCodeProvider (in Glims: system management->customize->GLIMS, field HC Code provider). The IK numbers of Kassen are assigned by the ssit_AlternateHCCodeProvider.
