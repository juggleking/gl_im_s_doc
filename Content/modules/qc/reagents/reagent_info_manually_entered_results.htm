Reagent info for manually entered results

Introduction

GLIMS also creates "ResultReagentUsage" records for results not originating from an analyzer. To prevent that GLIMS creates "ResultReagentUsage" records for reagent lots that are intended for manual result entry, a field Manual can be set for reagent usages.

When results are not received from an instrument but entered manually, GLIMS will create "ResultReagentUsage" records for the "manual" reagent usages currently valid for the result's assessment method.

Synchronizing data

The creation of "ResultReagentUsage" records for manually entered results was introduced in GLIMS v8.8.

Since reagent information and results might have been created before this new feature, some functions are available to synchronize results with manual reagent usages. One can do the synchronization on a Station, on a Result, and on a ReagentUsage.

The synchronization functions on Station and ReagentUsage have a "Start time" and "End time" parameter.

For performance reasons, GLIMS will remember the last synchronization time of a reagent usage. Deleting a ResultReagentUsage record will reset the LastSynchronisation field of the related ResultReagentUsage records.
