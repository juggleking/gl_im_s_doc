Family related browsers and editors

Introduction

This chapter is an overview of the Family browser. The modification of the data it contains as well as the creation of new records occurs in routine via the Family field of the Genetic exam screen and the Pedigree visualization of the Family screen.

Family

The family browser is available under Start -> Genetics -> Families. After selecting a family in this browser, you can double click to open the Pedigree visualization or use the contextual menu/ribbon items to open other Family record-related browsers and editors. These are listed in the following sections.

Note

Family records can only be deleted if they are not part of a genetic exam.

Family conditions

The Family conditions browser can be opened by clicking on the contextual menu/ribbon item Family conditions on a Family record in the Family browser. This browser is automatically populated when a condition is added for a disorder in the legend of the pedigree.

Disorder

Disorder to which the condition is linked in the legend of the pedigree

Label

Name of the condition in the legend

Drawing info

Indicates how the condition is represented in the pedigree

Main suspected disorder

The Main suspected disorder editor can be opened by clicking on the contextual menu/ribbon item Main suspected disorder on a Family record in the Family browser. It is populated when a Main suspected disorder is indicated in the Family screen.

Genetic exams

This will open a browser containing all Genetic exams related to this family record.
