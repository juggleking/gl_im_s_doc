Prenatal context

The new Genetics module supports the execution of genetic tests on pregnant women and their foetuses. This page describes the specific features of this prenatal context.

Order

When specimens and genetic procedures are requested for a pregnant mother and her foetus(es), a single order is created. The order's object (patient) is the mother. All the elements of the order are linked to the mother: the requests, the (foetal) specimens, the results, the reports.

Genetic exam

Each genetic exam in the order is created for both the mother and her foetuses. As a consequence, in the Genetic exam screen, the Specimen pane shows all the specimens of the mother and her foetuses.

Specimens

In the Specimen info screen, the Prenatal option allows you to indicate that the specimen was collected on a foetus. You can then select or create a Person record marked as a Foetus.

Person records for foetus and child

Foetuses are registered in GLIMS as Person object records

  • whose Mother field is filled in, and
  • on which the option Foetus on creation is activated.

After the birth, the Person record originally created for the foetus is used for the child. The Foetus on creation field remains activated to indicate that when the Person record was created, the person was a foetus.

Note

This is different from the older system developed for genetic purposes: in this system a new Person record had to be created for the born child and linked to the foetus Person record.

The way the person records are displayed on the screens can be customised by means of a site function for person externalisations.

Person merge

Two foetuses or one foetus and a born child may be merged if their Genotypic gender and Mother are identical.

Results

The test results are reported on a single report.

Billing

All the tests are billed together. The patient for the billing is the mother.
