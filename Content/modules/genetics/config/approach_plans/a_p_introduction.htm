Introduction

Approach plans are the core elements of the Genetics module. They enable you to define step by step the full work flow of the laboratory for a given genetic procedure. This work flow includes the analytical strategy according to

  • the technical actions to be performed,
  • genes (loci) to analyse,
  • the clinical context,
  • the patient status.
