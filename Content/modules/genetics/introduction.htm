Introduction

The Genetics module is available under Start > Genetics. It is entirely dedicated to the work flows of Genetics labs for human genome analyses.

It enables users to

  • create and request all kinds of genetic analyses and the materials needed for their execution.
  • configure the steps of the genetic analyses, from pre-analytic to post-analytic,
  • follow and monitor ongoing analyses,
  • register patient genetic data and results,
  • create and update family data, including pedigrees,
  • edit genetic specific work lists,
  • create reports of the performed analyses and their results,
  • bill the performed analyses.
