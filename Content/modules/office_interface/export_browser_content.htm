Send browser information to MS Word or MS Excel

In all non-generated browsers (recognizable by the fact that they usually take the whole available height of the screen and they have an 'options' button), it is possible to send the whole contents of the browser to the external Microsoft Office programs 'Word' and 'Excel'.

To do this you should hit 'CTRL-O' while being positioned in the browser itself. This functionality however is part of the 'Office Application Link' module which is to be ordered separately.
