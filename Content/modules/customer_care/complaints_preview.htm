Complaints: Preview

This function allows to generate a report preview in PDF format without any intermediate screens.

PDF reports can only be generated if a template "complaint.jasper" (with subreport "complaint_subreport1.jasper") is available in the Glims "dat" folder. Those templates will be delivered as "complaint-sample.jasper" and "complaint_subreport1-sample.jasper" and should be further customized and renamed by the customer.
