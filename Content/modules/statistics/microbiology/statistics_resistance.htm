Statistics -> Resistance statistics

Contents: Introduction - Layout screen - Selection screen - Output examples

Introduction

Resistance statistics give an overview on the resistance of the isolated microorganisms. It allows to answer questions like:

  • What is the average resistance to a certain antibiotic ? To all antibiotics ? Which antibiotic yields the least resistance ?
  • Are there differences in resistance between certain wards ?
  • Is there an evolution in the resistance of certain microorganisms ?
  • ...

Objects/patients

Infections

Will group the antibiotic results according to the isolation series of the isolation (that was tested in the antibiogram).  This will result in e.g. a row per isolation series showing the actual series.

First isolations

Will group the antibiotic results according to the first isolation in the isolation series of the isolation (that was tested in the antibiogram).  "First" means chronologically by sampling time. This will result in e.g. a row per isolation series showing the first isolation.
