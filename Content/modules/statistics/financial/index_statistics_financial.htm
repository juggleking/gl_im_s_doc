Statistics -> Financial statistics

Financial statistics give an overview on the turnover of the lab and consist of two parts:

  1. Invoice statistics, which are based on the table of invoices
  2. Billing item statistics, which are based on the table of billing items
  3. Invoice summary statistics, which are base on the table of invoice summaries
  4. Invoice item statistics, which are based on the table of invoice items
  5. Payment statistics, which are based on the table of payments

They can be started from the main menu under Statistics -> Financial. For batch runs the function to use is StatisticsFinancial.
