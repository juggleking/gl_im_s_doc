Statistics -> Blood selection statistics

Blood selection statistics give an overview of the amount of blood selections and allows to answer questions as e.g.

  • How many blood selections of a specific blood product are in status reserved ?
  • How many blood selections are there with a minimum urgency level 2 per department and per recipient ?
  • How many blood selections were there last year per cross match value ?

Repeat count values

The number of times a blood selection has been discontinued and repeated.

Characteristic options are:

Occurrence

The occurrence of blood selections, with as possible calculations: frequency, relative frequency per row or column, and the presence or absence

Repeat count

With calculations minimum, maximum, average, sum, first, last

Occurrence + repeat count

Is the number of times a blood selection was repeated including the current blood selection itself, with calculations minimum, maximum, average, sum, first, last.

The selection screen

The time selection is based on the specified limits of the order receipt time associated with the blood selection (Received from .. to ..). The receipt time is also the type of time for classification (see Lay-out screen).

Other selection criteria are:

  • Status
  • Blood product
  • Expiration time (lower and upper bounds)
  • Minimal urgency
  • An additional MISPL filter.
