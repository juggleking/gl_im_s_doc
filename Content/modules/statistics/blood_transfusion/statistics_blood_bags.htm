Statistics -> Blood bag statistics

Blood bag statistics give detailed information of the amount or the characteristics of blood bags in the lab. It allows to answer questions like:

  • How many blood bags per department have been processed in each month of last year ?
  • How many blood bags per batch have been delivered to the lab ?
  • What percentage of blood bags have been returned ?
  • How many blood bags caused an incident during transfusion last month ?

The selection screen

The selection screen features the same options as the first tab folder of the blood bag query in GLIMS. The second tab folder of this query (see below), is of no use for the statistics as the options are too specific.

Time options

On top of the selection screen, you can choose what kind of time reference you would like to use. This is a new concept, only available in the blood bag statistics and the blood bag browser. The time options are receipt time and transfusion time.  It is important to realize that this choice will also inflect the time classifications in the lay-out screen!

Selecting receipt time will result in statistics containing all blood bags that have been received by your lab within the specified in time limits (of course you can add other selection options as well). Selecting transfusion time will result in statistics containing blood bags that have been transfused within the specified in time limits only.  In that case the blood bag status is set to Administered, no others are possible.

Further selection options

  • You can select blood bags of a specific blood product, specification, blood group or rhesus.
  • Blood bags with a status between the two status levels that you select.
  • Blood bags linked to a specific department, manufacturer or supplier.
  • Select only normal, predestined or autologous blood bags (donation type).
  • Returned bags and incidental bags can be included, excluded or selected.
  • Finally you can run an additional MISPL-filter on the selection.
