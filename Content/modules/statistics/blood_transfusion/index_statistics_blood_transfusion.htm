Statistics -> Blood transfusion statistics

BTM statistics give an overview of what has happened to blood bags and blood selections in the lab. It contains:

  Blood bag statistics (based on the BloodBag table)
  Blood selection statistics (based on the BloodSelection table)

They can be started from the main menu: Site -> Transfusion Management -> Statistics.  As it is based on the general statistics frame, its usage is analogous to other statistics.
