Genetics statistics

Genetics statistics give an overview on the genetic exams of the lab and consist of:

  1. Genetic exams statistics, which are based on the table of genetic exams
  2. Locus results statistics, which are based on the table of locus results
  3. Variant results statistics, which are based on the table of variant results

They can be accessed via the main menu under Statistics > Genetics. For batch runs, the function is StatisticsGenetics.
