Merging purchase orders

Introduction

The stock management module offers a function to merge purchase orders in status "scheduled" that all belong to the same supplier, work place and stock.

Merge screen

The function to merge purchase orders can be executed in single-record mode or in multiple record mode. In both cases, GLIMS will first display a screen where the target purchase order (doc no, creation time/user and urgency) is displayed on top (read-only) and the purchase orders to be merged in an editing zone below, initially populated with the selected purchase orders to be merged.

The merge screen allows to delete purchase orders from the list and to add scheduled purchase orders with the same supplier, stock and work place as the target purchase order.

A warning will be displayed if the urgency or delivery date of the selected purchase orders do not match, with a possibility to skip the purchase order or to allow it.

When the Merge button is pushed, the purchase orders will be merged without further user interaction.

Merge actions

When starting the merge, the target purchase order (the first selected one or the focused purchase order if none selected) will be maintained, while the other purchase orders will be deleted, after merging their data.

  • The internal and external comments will be concatenated.
  • The purchase items of the duplicate purchase orders will be moved to the target purchase order. Purchase items of the same product specification, lot number and minimal expiration date will be grouped together. The purchase item external comments will be concatenated.
  • A purchase order log record mentioning the merged purchase orders will be created. The details of each purchase item will also be logged.
