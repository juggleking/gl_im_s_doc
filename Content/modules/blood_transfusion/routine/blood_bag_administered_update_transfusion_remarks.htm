BTM - Update transfusion remarks for administered blood bags

In the blood bag editor, the textual transfusion reactions and transfusion advice can no longer be updated once the blood bag is administered. To update transfusion reactions and advice for administered blood bags, a function Update commentary fields of an administered transfusion is available in the Blood bag contextual menu.

Update transfusion reactions

Allows updating the text saved in the field Reactions (textual) (BloodBag.TransfusionReactions) of the Administered tab in the Blood bag editor. If the option Copy data to PMR is enabled, the text will also be saved in the Person Medical Record.

Update blood bag comment

Allows updating the text saved in the field Comment (BloodBag.Comment) of the tab Received in the Blood bag editor.

Update transfusion advice

Transfusion advice is not stored in the blood bag info, but in the Person Medical Record. The already available transfusion advice is extended with the text specified in the Append field.
