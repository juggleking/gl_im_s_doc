BTM - Blood product specifications

Introduction

Blood product specifications allow to further subdivide the blood products. The barcodes of the specifications are fixed and are determined by the ISBT (International Society of Blood Transfusion).

Blood product specifications browser and editor

The Blood product specifications browser can be opened via Start -> Transfusion management -> Configuration -> Blood product specifications. Double-click on a record to open its editor.

General fields

Barcode

When looking up a blood product specification (e.g. in the scan or disk import program), GLIMS will check for a blood product specification with

  • An identical barcode with prefix '&<'
  • An identical barcode without prefix
  • An alternative code without prefix (see below)

Mnemonic

Modifier / Prepare condition / Usage / Radiated / Leucocyte concentration / Component information / Donor merge / Aferese / Quarantine / Attained volume

Informational fields, currently not used by the software

Disabled

If set, the blood bag scan program and the blood bag diskette import programs will no longer use this blood product specification.
