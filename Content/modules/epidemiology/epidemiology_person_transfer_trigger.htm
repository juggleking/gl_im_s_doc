Epidemiology - Person transfer trigger

A "Person transfer" MISPL trigger can be defined in the 'Specific site' settings.   You can find it via System management -> Settings -> GLIMS general (tab folder 'Triggers') or via System management -> Settings -> GLIMS microbiology (tab folder 'General').

This trigger is executed on a temp-table 'w_StayChange' with following fields:

  • Person
  • OldStay
  • NewStay

The 'Person' field is always available. Either 'OldStay' or 'NewStay' will be available, according to the circumstances.

The trigger is executed whenever a person's location (stay) changes.   The following cases can be distinguished:

  1. A person is admitted into the hospital: a new encounter is created and a new stay is created (available through 'NewStay'); 'OldStay' will be unknown.
  2. A person is transferred from one ward/room to another ward/room: in this case 'OldStay' and 'NewStay' will refer the applicable stay records.
  3. A person leaves the hospital: in this case the last stay of the encounter is closed (it gets an end date); this last stay will be available through 'OldStay' and 'NewStay' will be unknown.
