Epidemiology - Excluding wards

Introduction

The browsers on

  • Cross infections
  • Isolation series
  • Hospital infections
  • Cross infections of hospital infection
  • Isolation series of hospital infection

all offer the possibility to EXCLUDE a group of wards (as correspondent group) of which the cross infections have to be IGNORED. The wards that are excluded are shown at the bottom of the browsers.

Activation

When the browsers are started, the initial value for this criterion is fetched from the user preferences. This setting can only be changed via the 'Tools -> User preferences' program.

Remarks

  • For the cross infection browsers, this filter applies to the 'Via ward' field.
  • For the hospital infection browsers, a hospital infection will only be shown when it has at least one cross infection in a ward outside this group.
  • For the 'Isolation series' browsers, an isolation series will only be shown if either its 'From patient' or its 'Toward patient' infection is for a ward outside this group.
