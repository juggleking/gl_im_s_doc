Electronic order scheduler - Introduction

The Electronic Order Scheduler module in Glims keeps track of unscheduled orders planned for possible future examinations. This module requires a separate license named "GLIMS_EOS".

The general concept is to put orders for the future on hold (in status "Pending") and activate them later on (e.g. when specimens are sampled).
