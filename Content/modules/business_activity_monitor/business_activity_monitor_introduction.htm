Business Activity Monitor - Introduction

The Business Activity Monitor (BAM) provides an overview of the laboratory's current performance so that problems can quickly be detected. It shows information on the progress of recent orders, results and actions. It also indicates if orders and results are overdue.

The Business Activity Monitor is composed of two screens:

  • The General monitor provides data about current orders, results and actions for the whole laboratory, a specific department and/or property classification.
  • The Turnaround time (TAT) monitor provides an overview of the overall punctuality with which today's results have been confirmed and validated.

Note

If you use the Shielding program, note that shielding does not apply to record counts.

In order to use the Business Activity Monitor, some configuration is necessary:

  • Before the General monitor and TAT monitor can display any data, Monitor configurations must be created. They are parameter sets that can be configured in order to show BAM data for a specific department and/or property classification.
  • For the TAT monitor to display any data, confirmation and validation time frames must be specified in the configuration editors of properties.
  • In the BAM general settings, the layout, refresh rates and gauge maximal values of the General monitor can be customized.

  • In case of non-optimal performance, the debugging settings can help you find and solve the issue.
