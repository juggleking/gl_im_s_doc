Business Activity Monitor - Property TAT

Introduction

For each property, it is possible to define confirmation and validation turnaround time (TAT) limits. These limits are time frames within which the results of the property should be confirmed and validated.

The specification of TAT limits for properties is necessary to

  • See, in the TAT monitor, how many results corresponding to these properties have been confirmed/validated in time or too late.
  • Get an overview, in the TAT monitor, of how much time in average the confirmation and validation of results took.
  • See, in the General monitor, how many orders have overdue results and on which station these results are to be obtained.

Configuration of property TAT limits

In order to set TAT limits for a property:

  1. open the editor of this property
  2. click the More tab

  3. fill in the following fields:

    Maximal time till confirmation

    Maximal time laps (in hours and minutes) between a specimen's receipt time and the confirmation time of the results of this property.

    Maximal time till validation

    Maximal time laps (in hours and minutes) between a specimen's receipt time and the validation time of the results of this property.

Definition of overdue results

For orders and properties

Results are considered as overdue if they

  1. are not confirmed and the Maximal time till confirmation is exceeded.
  2. have been confirmed but later than allowed by the Maximal time till confirmation.
  3. are not validated and the Maximal time till validation is exceeded.
  4. have been validated but later than allowed by the Maximal time till validation.

For actions

Results are considered as overdue if they

  1. are not confirmed and the Maximal time till confirmation is exceeded.
  2. have been confirmed but later than allowed by the Maximal time till confirmation.
  3. are not validated and the Maximal time till validation is exceeded.
Note

Note

For actions, results which have been validated but whose validation took place once the Maximal time till validation was exceeded are NOT counted as overdue results.
