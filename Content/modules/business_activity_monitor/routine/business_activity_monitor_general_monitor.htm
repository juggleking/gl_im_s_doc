Business Activity Monitor - General monitor

Introduction

The General monitor provides data about current orders, results and actions for the whole laboratory, a specific department and/or property classification. It is available under Start -> Business activity monitor -> General monitor.

Components of the General monitor

The General monitor looks as follows:

It is composed of seven panes: Monitor, Order completeness, Performance, Instrument status, Overdue orders, By status, Date and time.

Monitor

The Monitor pane provides a selection list of monitor configurations for which BAM data can be displayed.

Order completeness

The Order completeness pane provides an overview of the progress made with the orders of the last two days.

Which orders are counted up?

The orders counted are those to which all of the following conditions apply:

  • their receipt time is today's / yesterday's / the day before yesterday's date,
  • their status is Initial, Closed or in between,
  • they have results.

If the monitor configuration selected in the Monitor pane refers to

  • a department, then only orders that were executed in this department are counted.
  • a property classification, then only the orders which have results whose property belongs to this classification are counted.

Sections of the completeness bars

Each completeness bar (Today, Yesterday, Two days ago) is divided into up to three sections:

  • Awaiting results:

    Number of orders which have at least one result in status Expected or Initial. Click on the number to consult these orders.

  • Awaiting validation:

    Number of orders which have at least one result in status Available or Confirmed. All results of the order have passed the Expected status. Click on the number to consult these orders.

  • Complete:

    Number of orders of which all results are validated.

Refresh rates of the completeness bars

The refresh rate of each completeness bar is individually configurable via the BAM general settings.

Performance

The Performance pane displays two gauges:

  • Orders:
    • indicates the number of orders created in the last 60 minutes.
    • If the monitor configuration selected in the Monitor pane refers to a department, only orders that were executed in this department are counted.
  • Results:
    • indicates the number of results of which the availability time (time at which the result was entered) is situated in the last 60 minutes.
    • If the monitor configuration selected in the Monitor pane refers to
      • a department, only results obtained in this department are counted.
      • a property classification, then only results of which the property belongs to this classification are counted.

The maximal value of both gauges can be modified via the BAM general settings.

If the monitor configuration selected in the Monitor pane refers to

  • a department, then only actions executed in this department will be counted
  • a property classification, then only the actions that are linked to a procedure with an output in this classification are counted.

Actions with overdue results

When results of an action are overdue, then the name of the station on which the action is executed is displayed in yellow or red:

  • yellow: at least one of the result outputs is overdue.

  • red: the average TAT of the results exceeds the weighted average of the property TAT limits.

    Example

    Example

    The following example illustrates the concept of weighted average:

    Say 3 results should be validated within 40 minutes and 2 results within 50 minutes. The weighted average of the maximal validation times is 44 minutes:

    3 results x 40 min = 120 min

    2 results x 50 min = 100 min

    120 + 100 = 220 min

    220 / 5 = 44 min

In both cases, clicking the name of the station enables the user to consult the overdue results.

Overdue orders

The Overdue orders pane shows, for the last two days, the number of orders being in time or overdue.

Which orders are counted up?

The orders counted are those to which all of the following conditions apply:

  • their receipt time is today's / yesterday's / the day before yesterday's date,
  • their status is Initial, Closed or in between,
  • they have results,
  • they have specimens.

If the monitor configuration selected in the Monitor pane refers to

  • a department, then only orders that were executed in this department are counted.
  • a property classification, then only the orders which have results whose property belongs to this classification are counted.

Sections of the bars

Each bar (Today, Yesterday, Two days ago) is divided into up to three sections:

  • In time:

    Number of orders of which all results are currently in time.

  • Unspecified:

    Number of orders of which none of the results have TAT limits defined.

  • Overdue:

    Number of orders which have at least one overdue result. Click on the number to consult these orders.

By status

Orders

Number of today's orders which are in status Initial, Partial and Complete.

Results

Number of results (of today's orders) which are in status Initial and Expected, Available and Confirmed and Validated.

Phone

Number of today's orders having at least one result for which the phone status is "To be phoned" (To do) and "Phoned" (Done).

Customization of the General monitor

The layout, refresh rates and gauge maximal values of the General monitor can be customized in the BAM general settings.
