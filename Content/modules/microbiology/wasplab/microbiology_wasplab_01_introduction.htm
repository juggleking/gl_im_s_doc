This chapter describes the communication flow between GLIMS and WASPLab (full microbiology lab automation).

Basic principles

  • GLIMS and WASPLab communicate in SMT mode (single medium).
  • In communication messages, microbiology actions are always identified by their grafting property (inbound / outbound code).
  • There will always be 1 message per microbiology action.
  • WASPLab takes the initiative in the communication with GLIMS.

Terminology

  • Plate = Carrier / Medium
  • Streaking / Inoculation = Grafting
  • Incubation = Growth
  • Segregation = Isolation
  • Antibiotic susceptibility test = Antibiotic result
