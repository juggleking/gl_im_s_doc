When grafting is successful, WASPLab sends the plates to an incubator.
At regular intervals, WASPLab (the incubator) will check the carriers for growth detection and informs GLIMS about this.

WASPLab

WASPLab regularly sends growth messages. The growth message contains:

  • A status line for each carrier.
  • A general status line that applies to the microbiology action.

GLIMS

GLIMS stores the info in the growth messages in its database.

Status line for each carrier

WASPLab GLIMS carrier field
Time offset (e.g. 8h) Growth observation time
Result status: P(reliminary) or F(inal) Growth status

Growth result:

  • N = Negative
  • P = Positive
  • C = Contaminated
  • U = Unclassified

General status line
