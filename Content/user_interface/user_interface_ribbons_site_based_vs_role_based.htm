Choosing between site-based or role based ribbons

Before starting the actual ribbon configuration, it is important to make a well considered choice between a site-based ribbon or role-based ribbons as this will have its effect on the complexity of the ribbon setup.

Site ribbon

Choosing for a site ribbon means choosing for the "one size fits all" solution.  A single ribbon will be used for all GLIMS users.  Using user/role privileges, it possible to hide specific ribbon items from users that do not have the required privilege.

So in case of a site ribbon, privileges are the only means to customize the ribbon for different users.

Role ribbons

If privileges are too confined to customize the ribbon for different user types, role-based ribbons are the solution.  They allow to setup a completely different ribbon tailored to the needs of the role of the logged in user.  

To ease the configuration, the GLIMS ribbons support

  1. inheritance from the site ribbon
  2. reuse of ribbon tabs among different role-based ribbons
