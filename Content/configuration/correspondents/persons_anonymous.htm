Patient anonymity

In order to hide the real identity of a patient, GLIMS allows to create one or more anonymous aliases for that person.  

Configuration

  • Create a new person which will be the anonymous alias.
  • Specify the patient of which you want to hide the real identity in the Real identity field of the anonymous alias.

Consultation

The contextual menu/ribbon items Anonymous alias and Real identity allow to display the aliases/real identity of a given person.

Protection

  1. Defining a privilege for the Person.RealIdentity and Person.AnonymousAliases functions will hide this real identity for non-privileged users.  
  2. The Real identity field will automatically be hidden for users who do not have the privilege defined for Person.RealIdentity

Note

Defining a Field update privilege for the Person.RealIdentity field will protect this field for update.
