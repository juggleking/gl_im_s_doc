Logging during generation of electronic reports

Interactive generation of electronic report

When interactively generating electronic reports, GLIMS will produce an error log screen whenever something goes wrong (e.g. problems during expansion of dynamic text, etc).

When closing this window, another screen will be shown with the full report generation logging.  This screen will only be shown if at least one error occurred during the report generation.

You can click the Review button to examine the full logging of the report generation.

Automated generation of electronic reports

When generating electronic reports in batch (via commands and tasks), the report generation logging can be found in the related service logging.
