Electronic reports

Introduction

Glims supports different types of electronic reports. In most cases, electronic reports are formatted and distributed through drivers (translators). However, GLIMS also allows to generate reports directly (without the intervention from a driver) into the format of an external protocol (e.g. HPRIM ORU reports, disk reports such as Medidoc, etc.).

Electronic reports generated via a driver

In most cases, electronic reports are formatted and distributed through drivers (translators). These so-called "online" reports have a report template with Generator set to Online. On the Electronic page of the report generation screen, you can specify all the related settings for these "online" reports.

The content and structure of the report messages depend on the driver used. For more information, please see the driver-specific documentation.

Electronic reports generated directly from Glims

Glims also allows to generate reports directly (without the intervention from a driver) into the format of an external protocol.  These kind of reports have a report template with Generator set to the specific protocol (e.g. "HPRIM1_2").

Report protocols that can be generated directly from GLIMS are:

  • HPRIM ORU
  • HL7 ORU
  • Edifact
  • Disk reports
  • Variable text reports (e.g. Tacamed)
