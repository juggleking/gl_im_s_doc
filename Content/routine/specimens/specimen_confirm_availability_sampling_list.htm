Confirm specimens by sampling list

Sampling lists are work lists containing actions without input and delivering only material outputs.

When the specimens on the sampling list have been gathered, their arrival is logged in the system using the Specimens to confirm method on the sampling list. This method produces a browser of expected specimens, all of which are pre-selected. After explicitly deselecting any missing specimens, the user can activate the Confirm availability method on the specimen selection. This will automatically promote the specimens from status 'Expected' to status 'Available'.
