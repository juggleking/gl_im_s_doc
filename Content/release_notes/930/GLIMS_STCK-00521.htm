Release notes 9.3.0 - Disallow creation/deletion of items for purchase orders with status > approved (GLIMS_STCK-00521)

In several stock management screens (product ordering, product check-in, etc), the buttons to add/delete new purchase order items are disabled when the purchase order has a status higher than Approved.

However, it was still possible to use the Insert / Delete keys of the keyboard. This has been corrected.
