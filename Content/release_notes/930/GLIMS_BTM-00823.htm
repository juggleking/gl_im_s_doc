Release notes 9.3.0 - Evaluate report expression when crossmatch specimen is available (GLIMS_BTM-00823)

In the blood transfusion module, when selecting a blood bag for a blood selection, GLIMS will automatically add a crossmatch requestable to the order (as configured in the blood product).

This crossmatch requestable requires a blood specimen. As there may be a significant delay between the creation of the original order and the availability of the specimen required for the crossmatch, GLIMS will now re-evaluate the Report expression (also defined in the general options) as soon as this specimen becomes Available.
