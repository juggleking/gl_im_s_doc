Release notes 9.3.0 - Extensions for reporting statistics by email (MATE_STAT-00147)

The "Output options" screen of the statistics modules allows to report the statistics by email. The following enhancements have been implemented:

  1. In previous versions, the "To" field was limited to 100 characters. This restriction has been lifted.
  2. The new "CC" field allows to specify who should receive a copy of the email.

  3. In both the "To" and "CC" field, you an use the F6 key to open a larger window.
