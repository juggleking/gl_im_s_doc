Release notes 9.9.3 - Disable caching of most frequently used tables (MATE-06520)

Up until now, when starting GLIMS, the most frequently used tables were cached. However, this optimization caused issues for which workarounds were implemented (for instance: MATE-04341). Unfortunately, these workarounds caused other issues. For instance, opening the quick report for an order when the quick invoice was already open did not work.

To avoid these issues, the most frequently used tables are now no longer cached when starting GLIMS.
