Release notes 9.9.3 - Financial export in KVDT format: different BESA and RVSA records for LG shipments (BILX_GKVDT-00418)

For LG KVDT shipments, the BESA and RVSA records will now be exported as follows:

  • The BESA record now contains one BSNR (the BSNR of the Firm which is also part of the file name) and no longer the BSNR of the internal departments.
  • The RVSA record now contains the BSNR of the Firm as well. The external issuers are no longer listed in the RVSA record.
