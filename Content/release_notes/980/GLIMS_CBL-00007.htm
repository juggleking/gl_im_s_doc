Release notes 9.8.0 -Enhancements for xml export of fund and policy related data to CyberLab (GLIMS_CBL-00007)

Fund and policy related data can be sent from GLIMS to CyberLab in an .xml file. The list of data elements that can be exported has been extended. The export file now contains

  • additional fields in the Fund section
  • a section with policy information
  • additional fields in the PolicyName section
  • a section with policy clause information

More detailed information is available in the documentation.
