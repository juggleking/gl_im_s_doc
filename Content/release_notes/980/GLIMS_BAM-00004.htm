Release notes 9.8.0 - Full stops and commas correctly read as decimal separators in refresh rates (GLIMS_BAM-00004)

Background

The Business Activity Monitor offers the possibility to customize the frequency at which data must be updated. To do so, it suffices to indicate the chosen rates in the XML code of the BAM general settings. These rates must be specified using decimal numbers, 0.1 hour for 6 minutes, for instance.

Issue and solution

Decimal numbers written with a decimal symbol that is different from the decimal symbol of the regional settings of the client computer were not interpreted correctly, giving rise to incorrect refresh rates.

This issue has been corrected: Both fullstops and commas are now correctly read as decimal separators, independently of the regional settings.
