Release notes 9.4.0 - Redesigned browsers (GLIMS-05873)

The following browsers have been re-designed:

  • Orders by internal id 
  • Orders by receipt time
  • Orders by short id, receipt time
  • Purchase items of Purchase order
  • Purchase items of Product specification

  • Purchase items of Product lot

Note

So-called Cycle columns (columns with a cycle icon in the header, to switch between different data) are no longer supported. You can now use the Field chooser to define which data should be shown.
