Release notes 9.9.0 - ASV, LANR and BSNR of German official issuers exported in LDT context (GLIMS_OX-00134)

Since GLIMS_OI-00784, GLIMS supports the German ASV, LANR and BSNR for official issuers. This data is stored in the Referral table and displayed in the Referral tab page in the Order entry screen.

While this data could be imported from electronic order messages, it was missing when orders were exported in the LDT context.

This issue has been fixed: the ASV, LANR and BSNR are now available under the form of an identification list in the export URL-message produced by GLIMS.
