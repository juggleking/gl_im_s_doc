Release notes 9.9.0 - No "Transfusion started" message when using a wasted blood bag (GLIMS_CBT-00264)

In CyberTrack, when

  • a transfusion was started with a wasted blood bag, and
  • the CyberTrack general setting Send emails about transf. reactions to was empty,

a message was displayed and wrongly indicated that the transfusion was started.

This has been corrected for CyberTrack 4.0.2 on GLIMS 9.8.6 and higher versions.
