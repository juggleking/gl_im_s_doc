Release notes 9.9.0 - Wasted and discontinued blood bags can be set back to status Initial (GLIMS_BTM-00929)

This modification introduces two new contextual ribbon/menu items on Blood bag records: Wasted to 'Initial' and Discontinued to 'Initial'. As their labels indicate, these items allow setting one or more selected blood bags in status Wasted or Discontinued back to status Initial.

When Discontinued to 'Initial' and Wasted to 'Initial' are used, the fields of the Discontinued tab in the blood bag editor are cleared. If the selected blood bag(s) had been checked out or returned, the fields of the Checked out or Returned tabs are also cleared.

Note

It is advised to reserve this functionality to specific users, using privileges.
