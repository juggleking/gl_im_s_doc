Release notes 9.9.0 - Correction of week of year calculation in Statistics module (MATE_STAT-00166)

In the Statistics module, the week of year calculation is now based on ISO 8601 / ISO week date.

Example

Up until now, the statistics output referred to 31/12/2019 - 6/1/2019 as week 0.

As of now, this will be week 1.

Up until now, the statistics output referred to 7/1/2019 -> 13/1/2019 as week 1.

As of now, this will be week 2.
