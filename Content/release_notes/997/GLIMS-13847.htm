Release notes 9.9.7 - Removed redundant lock statements in various v8 - v9 conversion procedures (GLIMS-13847)

Exclusive locks were used for the entirety of transactions in one of the conversion routines, with only a small section of the conversion requiring them. As a result, many locks could be held, eventually maxing out the start-up parameter "Use Lock Table Entries (-L)" and causing an error.

This issue, which occurred when upgrading from an older GLIMS version to version 9 or higher, has been corrected.
