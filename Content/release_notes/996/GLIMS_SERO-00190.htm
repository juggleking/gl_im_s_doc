Release notes 9.9.6 - Fixed issue where specimen scan left open positions (GLIMS_SERO-00190)

This modification fixes the issue where in certain circumstances the specimen scan program skipped the next available position when switching between racks.

Configuration

  1. A rack type with Use first free position deselected.
  2. An archive with 2 racks of the above rack type.

Scenario

  1. Scan a specimen into the 1st position of the first rack. The next position will be the 2nd position of the 1st rack.
  2. Scan a specimen into the last position of the first rack. The next position will be the 1st position of the 2nd rack.
  3. Scan a specimen into the 2nd position of the first rack. The next position will be the 1st position of the 2nd rack. It should be the 3rd position of the 1st rack.
