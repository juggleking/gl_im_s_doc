Release notes 9.5.0 - Issue with special system manager privileges (MATE-03689)

Background information

System managers have access to all fields, functions and tables. They also have the authority to assign privileges to users.

Exception: system managers will not have access to functions, tables or fields protected by the _SysAdm_NoPriv1 and _SysAdm_NoPriv2 privileges if these privileges are not explicitly assigned to them.

Issue

The _SysAdm_NoPriv1 and _SysAdm_NoPriv2 privileges were not checked when they were assigned to a role instead of to a user.

This has been corrected.
